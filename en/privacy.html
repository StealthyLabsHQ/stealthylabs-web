Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At StealthyLabs, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Our website operates on a strict no-log policy:

  • No personal information (name, address, phone number) is collected
  • No IP addresses are stored in our databases
  • No advertising trackers (Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, etc.) are used
  • No cookies that track your behavior across the web

3. Cookies

We use cookies only to save your display preferences:

  • Your selected language (English/French)
  • Your font preference

These cookies are strictly functional and are never used for tracking or shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site may embed content from third-party services (YouTube videos, Discord widgets, etc.). These services have their own privacy policies, and we have no control over the data they may collect.
